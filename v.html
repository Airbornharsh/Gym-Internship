ABOUT US

We believe that fitness should not be one dimensional and we know that you can maximise your full potential with an all-encompassing workout. Flying solo is great but striving together is better which is why we offer the highest possible variety at an affordable cost. At Total Fitness we encourage choice as well as regular exercise and we use our high variety to offer a ‘360 approach’ ensuring you are able to train the entire body. Your personal fitness is our priority and we understand the importance of variation in your workout.

OUR SERVICES

If you don’t know already, we’re super scale clubs with almost 100,000 members. We attract a wide range of members with memberships for kids to over 65’s, all who have made the important step to commit to their health and fitness. With such big spaces and a broad membership base, we’ve limited the number of personal trainers per club so you have the best chance of success. We operate with two personal training models. You can join us as an employed personal trainer with all the benefits as a member of staff and a clear progression journey if you are interested in working as a self employed personal trainer once you have your client base established. Or, you can join us as a partner working with us as a self employed personal trainer with access to our members and a competitive rent.
